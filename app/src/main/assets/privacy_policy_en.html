Privacy Policy - AdSkipper

Last updated: February 26, 2025

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how the "AdSkipper" application collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collected by the App

The application does not collect any personally identifiable information. We only collect anonymous information for service improvement:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (number of ads detected, applications where the app is active)
  • Error data and crash reports (without personal information)
  • Technical information about the device (OS version, device model) without unique identifiers

Use and Storage of Information

All collected information is stored exclusively on your device and is not shared with external servers or third parties. The data is used only for improving the application and solving errors.

Required Permissions

The application requests certain permissions to provide its service:

  • Accessibility services - to identify and skip ads
  • Display over other apps permission - to perform skip actions
  • Foreground service permission - for the app to continue running in the background
  • Notification permission - to display a notification while the service is active

Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the information stored on your device. Sensitive information in the application is encrypted using advanced encryption methods.

Use of Third-Party Applications

The application operates with other applications on your device, such as social media applications. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of these applications and we recommend that you review their privacy policies.

User Rights

You may at any time:

  • Delete all data stored by the application (through the application settings)
  • Stop using the application and remove it

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. In case of material changes, we will notify you through the application.
