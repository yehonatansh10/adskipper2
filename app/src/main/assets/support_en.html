Help and Support

Accessibility Permissions Warning

AdSkipper requires accessibility permissions to automatically detect and skip ads. While we only use these permissions for their intended purpose, they provide extensive access to screen content and interactions. We never collect, store, or transmit any data from your screen. All processing happens locally on your device for your privacy and security.

Troubleshooting Tips

If the app suddenly stops working correctly, try resetting the accessibility permissions. Go to your device Settings > Accessibility > AdSkipper, disable the service, then enable it again. This often resolves issues caused by system updates or resource constraints.
